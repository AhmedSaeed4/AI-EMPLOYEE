# Stop Throwing Money Away: 3 Automation Mistakes You're Making Right Now 🛑

Everyone talks about automation. But most businesses get it WRONG — and it's costing them time, money, and sanity.

Here are the 3 biggest automation mistakes I see (and how to fix them):

❌ **Mistake #1: Automating BAD processes**

Here's the truth: Automating a broken process just creates a faster broken process.

Before you automate anything, ask yourself: "Does this process actually WORK?"

Fix the process first. THEN automate it.

❌ **Mistake #2: The "Set It and Forget It" trap**

Automation needs monitoring. Things change. APIs break. Requirements evolve.

Set up alerts. Check in weekly. Your automated systems need checkups just like a car needs oil changes.

❌ **Mistake #3: Trying to automate everything at once**

Start small. Pick ONE repetitive task. Automate it. Test it. Improve it.

Then move to the next one.

The businesses that succeed with automation? They build it gradually, not overnight.

## Key Takeaways
- Fix broken processes BEFORE automating them
- Monitor your automations regularly
- Start small and scale gradually
