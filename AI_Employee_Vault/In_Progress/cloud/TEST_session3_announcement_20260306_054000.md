# Task: Social Media - Product/Service Announcement

**Task Type:** SOCIAL MEDIA POST

**Platform:** LinkedIn (primary), with adaptations for Twitter and Facebook

**Announcement:** New Service Offering - AI Automation Consulting

**Core Announcement:**
After 6 months of beta testing with select clients, you're officially launching AI Automation Consulting services for small and medium businesses.

**Key Details to Include:**
- **What:** AI Automation Consulting - helping businesses identify and implement automation opportunities
- **Target:** Small businesses (5-50 employees) wasting time on repetitive tasks
- **Offerings:** Process analysis, automation design, implementation, training
- **Results from Beta:** Average 12 hours/week saved across 8 beta clients
- **Investment:** Starting at $2,500 for initial automation audit + implementation plan
- **Timeline:** Most clients see ROI within 6-8 weeks

**Client Stories from Beta:**
- Client A: Saved 15 hours/week automating invoice follow-ups
- Client B: Reduced admin time by 40% with automated scheduling
- Client C: Scaled to handle 3x more clients without hiring

**Positioning:**
"AI isn't replacing jobs - it's freeing people to focus on what matters. We help businesses identify the repetitive tasks that can be automated and implement practical solutions that save time and money."

**Target Audience:**
- Small business owners feeling overwhelmed
- Companies growing but drowning in admin work
- Businesses wanting to scale without adding headcount
- Organizations interested in AI but unsure where to start

**Goals of This Announcement:**
- Generate 10+ qualified leads for the new service
- Build credibility with beta client results
- Differentiate from "AI hype" - this is practical automation
- Position as expert in AI for small business
- Test market response before full marketing push

**Tone:**
- Exciting but grounded (not hype-y)
- Results-focused with concrete numbers
- Accessible (not too technical)
- "Here's what actually works" authenticity

**Call-to-Action:**
- "DM me 'AUTOMATE' for a free 30-minute consultation to identify your top automation opportunities"
- Or link to booking calendar if available

**Visual Suggestion:**
Infographic showing "Before Automation" vs "After Automation" with time savings highlighted

**Hashtags:** #AIAutomation #SmallBusiness #Productivity #BusinessAutomation #WorkSmarter

**Platform Adaptations:**

### LinkedIn (Primary - Full Announcement)
- 1,500-2,000 characters
- Tell the full story
- Include client examples
- Professional tone with personality
- Focus on business impact

### Twitter (Thread Format)
- Break into 6-8 tweets
- Hook → Problem → Solution → Results → CTA
- Use 🔗 for thread connectors
- Punchy, stat-driven

### Facebook (Community-Focused)
- 300-400 characters
- Highlight small business impact
- "Finally, AI that actually helps small business"
- Community-focused tone

**Special Note:**
This is a soft launch announcement to your existing network. Full marketing push (ads, PR, etc.) comes later after initial feedback and refinements.

**Action Required:**
- Draft LinkedIn announcement post (primary)
- Include Twitter thread version
- Include Facebook post version
- All should tell consistent story but platform-optimized
- Lead with value, not features
- Back claims with beta client results
- Include clear CTA
- Suggest visual/creative to accompany

**Brand Voice:** Practical expertise, "we've tested this and it works", excitement grounded in real results, not making AI promises you can't deliver
