# You're Not "Too Small for AI." You're Too Small to Keep Wasting 15 Hours a Week.

There's a myth that AI employees are for big companies with big budgets.

In my experience building digital FTEs, the opposite is true. The smaller your team, the more every hour costs you — because every hour spent on repetitive work is an hour you're NOT spending on sales, strategy, or the work that actually grows revenue.

**5 quiet signs your business is ready for its first AI employee:**

1. You have a task you do the exact same way every single week
2. You've said "I'll get to that" for 3+ weeks on something important
3. Your data lives in 3+ tools that don't talk to each other
4. You're the bottleneck — nothing moves until you touch it
5. You pay for software but still do the work by hand

Notice the pattern? These aren't "AI problems." They're capacity problems. And a digital FTE solves them — no salary, no onboarding, no 8-hour days.

The businesses winning right now aren't the biggest. They're the ones who automated the boring stuff first and bought their time back.
