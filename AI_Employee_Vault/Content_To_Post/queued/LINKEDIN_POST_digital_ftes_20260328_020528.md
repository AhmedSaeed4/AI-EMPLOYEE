# Your Best Employee Never Sleeps (And Doesn't Ask for a Raise)

No, I'm not talking about you working 24/7. That's a burnout recipe.

I'm talking about **Digital FTEs** - AI employees that handle the work you don't have time for.

Here's what changes when you add a digital employee to your team:

**They work while you rest**
- Responding to inquiries at 2am
- Processing orders during your lunch break
- Following up with leads while you're in meetings

**They're consistent, not variable**
- No sick days
- No learning curve
- Same quality, every single time

**They scale instantly**
- Need 10x more output? No hiring process. No training. Just more compute.

But here's the mistake most businesses make:

They try to automate everything at once. Then it breaks. Then they quit.

**The right approach:**
1. Start with ONE task that drains your energy
2. Build the AI system for that specific workflow
3. Prove it works. Then expand.

One client I worked with started with just email triage. Their Digital FTE now handles 80% of their customer inquiries. They hired a human for the 20% that actually requires human judgment.

Result: Better customer experience. Lower costs. More time for strategy.

## Key Takeaways
- Start with one energy-draining task
- AI should handle volume; humans handle judgment
- Scale after proof, not before
