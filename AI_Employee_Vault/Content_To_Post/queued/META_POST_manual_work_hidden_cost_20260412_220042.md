# Your Business Is Bleeding Hours. Here's Where They're Going. 🩸

You think you're saving money by doing everything yourself.

Let's do the math on that.

Every week, the average small business owner spends:
- 5+ hours on email management and follow-ups
- 4+ hours creating and posting social media content
- 3+ hours on invoicing, bookkeeping, and admin
- 3+ hours chasing leads and responding to inquiries

That's 15+ hours a week on tasks that don't generate a single dollar of new revenue.

Over a month? 60+ hours. Over a year? 720+ hours.

**That's 90 full workdays** spent on things an AI employee could handle while you sleep.

Here's the shift most business owners miss: you don't need to hire a team to get your time back. You need a system that runs the repetitive 60% of your operations so you can focus on the work that actually grows your business.

A Digital FTE handles email sorting, lead follow-ups, content posting, invoice tracking, and reporting — all without breaks, sick days, or salary negotiations.

## Key Takeaways
- 720+ hours per year are lost to tasks that don't drive revenue
- You can't hire your way out of this — the math doesn't work for small teams
- A Digital FTE costs a fraction of one employee while covering multiple roles

The most successful solopreneurs I know aren't working more hours than you. They just stopped treating manual work like it's unavoidable.
