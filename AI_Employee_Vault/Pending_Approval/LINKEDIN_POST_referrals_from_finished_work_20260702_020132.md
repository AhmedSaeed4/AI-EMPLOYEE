# Your best lead source isn't ads or SEO. It's the clients you already finished work for.

Every project ends the same way: a "thank you," an invoice, and silence. The relationship dies right when it's most valuable.

Here's what's wild — a referred lead converts far more often than a cold one, and costs you nothing. Yet almost no business has a system for asking for that referral. Or the review. Or the testimonial. Or the intro.

So the highest-converting lead source you own gets treated like an accident.

The problem isn't that you don't know it matters. It's that asking is high-value, low-urgency work. Two weeks after delivery, it feels awkward. Three weeks, you forget. Six weeks, the client has moved on — and so has the moment.

This is exactly the gap a digital FTE fills. It makes the right ask at the right time, every time — the review request 48 hours after delivery, the referral nudge two weeks later, the "know anyone else who needs this?" check-in a month out. No awkwardness, no forgetting, no lead left on the table.

The clients most likely to send you business are the ones you've already made happy. Stop hoping they remember to.

## Key Takeaways
- Referred leads convert far better than cold ones — and they cost nothing to acquire
- Most businesses never systematize the ask, so referrals stay accidental
- A digital FTE handles the high-value, low-urgency follow-up humans quietly skip
