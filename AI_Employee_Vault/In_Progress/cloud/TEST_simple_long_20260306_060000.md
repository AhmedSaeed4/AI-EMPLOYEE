**Current Situation:**

Our existing website was developed in 2019 and no longer meets our needs or the expectations of our patients. Additionally, we need to integrate with newer healthcare systems and comply with updated accessibility requirements.

**Project Requirements:**

**1. Core Website Development:**

We need a new website with the following sections and functionality:

- **Home Page:** Welcoming, professional design that clearly communicates who we are and what we offer
- **About Us:** Organization history, mission, values, leadership team bios, community involvement
- **Locations:** Detailed information for all seven locations including addresses, maps, hours, contact information, and photos
- **Services:** Comprehensive listing of all medical services we provide, organized by specialty and service type
- **Providers:** Directory of all 25 healthcare providers with photos, bios, specialties, education, and appointment booking
- **Patient Resources:** Educational content, forms, insurance information, patient portal access
- **News & Updates:** Health articles, clinic announcements, seasonal health tips
- **Contact:** General contact form, location finder, emergency information
- **Careers:** Job listings, company culture, benefits, application process

**2. Patient Portal Integration:**

We use a third-party patient management system (EHR) and need to integrate:
- Patient login from our website to their portal
- Appointment request form that syncs with our scheduling system
- Prescription refill requests
- Secure messaging with providers
- Access to test results and health records

**3. Online Appointment Scheduling:**

Patients should be able to:
- Search for available appointments by location, provider, service type, and date/time
- Book appointments online without calling
- Receive confirmation emails with appointment details
- Get appointment reminders via email and SMS
- Cancel or reschedule appointments online

**4. Insurance Information:**

We need to display:
- List of insurance plans we accept (approximately 40 plans across 7 states)
- Insurance verification tool where patients can check their coverage
- Information about copays, deductibles, and out-of-pocket costs
- Financial assistance and payment plan information

**5. Content Management:**

Our marketing team needs to be able to:
- Easily update content without technical knowledge
- Add/edit/delete pages
- Upload images and documents
- Manage provider profiles and bios
- Post news articles and health tips
- Update location information and hours

**6. Search Functionality:**

The site must have robust search that allows patients to find:
- Providers by name, specialty, location, or language spoken
- Services and treatments
- Locations and hours
- Insurance plans accepted
- Health education content

**7. Accessibility Compliance:**

We must comply with:
- WCAG 2.1 AA standards for web accessibility
- HIPAA requirements for protected health information
- Section 508 requirements for government accessibility
- Language access for limited English proficiency patients

**8. Multi-Language Support:**

We serve diverse communities and need:
- English and Spanish versions of the entire site
- Easy language toggle in the header
- Professional medical translations (not automated)
- Culturally appropriate imagery and content

**9. Performance & Technical Requirements:**

- Fast loading times (under 3 seconds on mobile)
- Mobile-responsive design (60% of our traffic is mobile)
- SEO-optimized for local search in our service areas
- Secure hosting with SSL certificate
- Regular security updates and backups
- Analytics integration to track user behavior
- Integration with our existing CRM (Salesforce Health Cloud)

**10. Design Considerations:**

Our brand guidelines include:
- Color palette: Navy blue (#1E3A5F), teal (#4ECDC4), white, light gray
- Typography: Clean, professional sans-serif fonts
- Imagery: Diverse patients and providers, warm and welcoming
- Overall tone: Professional, trustworthy, accessible, caring
- Avoid overly clinical or sterile appearance
