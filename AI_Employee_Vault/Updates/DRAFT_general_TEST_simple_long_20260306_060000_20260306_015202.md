## Responses to Your Questions

**1. Do you have experience developing websites for healthcare organizations or medical practices?**

Yes, we have worked with healthcare clients on website development projects. We understand the importance of creating sites that are both functional and compassionate, serving as a digital extension of the care you provide. Our approach emphasizes accessibility, clarity, and ease of navigation—key factors that contribute to patient satisfaction.

**2. What's your recommended technology stack for a project like this?**

For a project of this scope with complex integrations, we recommend:
- **Frontend:** A modern framework such as React or Vue.js, supported by a headless CMS
- **Backend:** Node.js or Python-based solution, depending on integration requirements
- **CMS:** WordPress or a headless CMS like Contentful or Sanity, tailored for ease of use by your marketing team
- **Database:** Scalable solution compatible with your integration needs
- **EHR Integration:** API-first approach using Epic's FHIR endpoints or other applicable integration methods

We would refine this recommendation during the discovery phase based on a deeper technical assessment of your existing systems and long-term goals.

**3. How do you handle HIPAA compliance for patient data?**

HIPAA compliance is non-negotiable in healthcare projects. Our approach includes:
- **Data Minimization:** We avoid storing or transmitting protected health information (PHI) unless absolutely necessary
- **Secure Transmission:** All data transmission uses TLS 1.2+ encryption
- **Access Controls:** Role-based access and strict authentication protocols
- **Business Associate Agreement (BAA):** We execute a BAA with all healthcare clients to formalize our compliance commitments
- **Risk Assessments:** We conduct regular security assessments and follow industry best practices

For your project, patient portal access and any PHI-related functionality would integrate directly with your Epic Systems instance, minimizing PHI exposure on our end.

**4. Can you integrate with our existing EHR system (Epic Systems)?**

Yes, Epic Systems integration is feasible. Epic provides APIs (including FHIR-based endpoints) that allow for secure integration with third-party websites. We would work with your IT team to:
- Understand the specific Epic modules and APIs available at your organization
- Configure secure API connections for patient portal access, appointment scheduling, and prescription refill requests
- Ensure all integration points comply with Epic's requirements and your organization's security protocols

During discovery, we would review your specific Epic configuration and integration requirements in detail.

**5. What's your approach to accessibility and multi-language support?**

**Accessibility (WCAG 2.1 AA & Section 508):**
- Design and development with accessibility as a foundational requirement, not an afterthought
- Semantic HTML structure, keyboard navigation, and proper ARIA labeling
- Screen reader compatibility and focus management
- Color contrast compliance and text scalability
- User testing with assistive technologies during development
- Post-launch accessibility audit

**Multi-Language Support (English/Spanish):**
- Implementation of a language switcher in the header as requested
- Separate content trees for each language to allow for nuanced, culturally appropriate translations
- Recommendation of professional medical translation services we can coordinate with
- Proper hreflang tags for SEO
- Language-specific URL structure for clarity and maintainability

**6. What does your project management process look like?**

We use an agile project management approach with regular communication and milestone checkpoints:

- **Dedicated Project Manager:** A single point of contact who coordinates all work and keeps you informed
- **Weekly Status Meetings:** Scheduled check-ins to review progress, address questions, and plan ahead
- **Project Dashboard:** Access to a shared portal (e.g., Asana, Monday.com, or similar) for transparency on tasks and timelines
- **Milestone Reviews:** Formal reviews at key phases (design approval, development milestones, testing, launch readiness)
- **Change Management:** Clear process for scope changes with impact analysis before implementation

Communication is a priority—we believe in proactive updates and responding to questions promptly.

**7. How do you handle content migration from our existing site?**

- **Content Audit:** We'll catalog all existing content and identify what should be migrated, updated, or archived
- **Content Mapping:** We'll map legacy content to the new site structure
- **Quality Review:** We'll identify outdated or inconsistent content that needs refresh before migration
- **Migration Execution:** We'll handle the technical migration of text and approved media assets
- **Validation:** Post-migration review to ensure accuracy and formatting integrity

We would also provide guidance on content optimization for better user experience and SEO performance.

- **Refresher Training:** Option for additional training sessions as needed
