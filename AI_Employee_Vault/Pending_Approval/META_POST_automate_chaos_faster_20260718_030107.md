# Unpopular opinion: AI won't fix your broken process. It'll just break it faster. ⚡

Most businesses rush to "add AI" hoping it'll cure the chaos — the messy handoffs, the forgotten follow-ups, the leads that slip through the cracks.

Here's the harsh truth: automation is an amplifier, not a fixer.

A clunky 12-step process doesn't become elegant when you automate it. It becomes a clunky 12-step process running at 3am — faster, sure, but just as broken. And now the mess is hidden inside a bot where nobody can see it.

The smart sequence isn't "automate everything." It's:

1. Map the workflow honestly
2. Cut the steps that exist only because "that's how we've always done it"
3. THEN let your AI employee run the clean version

Automate chaos and you get faster chaos. Simplify first, automate second, and you get leverage that actually compounds. 📈

That's the whole difference between a Digital FTE that saves you hours every week and one that just burns them faster.

## Key Takeaways
- AI amplifies whatever workflow you feed it — good or bad
- Automating a messy process = faster mess, now hidden from view
- Simplify the workflow first; let AI run the clean version

What's one step in your process that's more complicated than it needs to be? Drop it below 👇 and I'll tell you straight: automate it, simplify it, or kill it.
