# Your Content Is Being Skipped (Here's Why)

Every day, millions of videos get uploaded.

Most get scrolled past in under 2 seconds.

The difference between content that stops the scroll and content that gets skipped isn't luck. It's structure.

After creating countless short-form videos, I've noticed a pattern:

**The winners follow a 3-part formula:**

1. **Pattern Interrupt** - The first 1-2 seconds must break expectations. Movement, color shift, unexpected opening line. If they predict what's coming, they scroll.

2. **Immediate Value Hook** - Don't build up to the point. Lead with it. "Here's how to double your leads" beats "Today I want to talk about..."

3. **Visual Rhythm** - Cuts every 2-3 seconds, text overlays, motion graphics. Static content = bored viewers.

But here's what most people miss:

The best short-form content isn't about being entertaining. It's about being useful in an entertaining way.

Educate while you captivate.

## Key Takeaways
- Lead with your strongest point, not your intro
- Plan 3 pattern interrupts per 30-second video
- Every cut should serve the message, not just fill time
