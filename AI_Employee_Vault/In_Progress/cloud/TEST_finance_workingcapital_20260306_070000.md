# Task: Weekly Working Capital Report

**Task Type:** FINANCE - WORKING CAPITAL

**Report Period:** Week of March 3-7, 2026

**Purpose:**
Generate a weekly working capital report to monitor short-term financial health, optimize cash conversion cycle, and ensure adequate working capital for operations.

**Report Sections Needed:**

**1. Working Capital Summary**
- Current assets (cash, receivables, inventory)
- Current liabilities (payables, short-term debt)
- Net working capital
- Working capital ratio
- Health assessment

**2. Cash Management**
- Cash on hand
- Cash equivalents
- Available credit facilities
- Total liquid resources
- Reserve adequacy

**3. Accounts Receivable Analysis**
- Total receivables outstanding
- Aging breakdown (current, 1-30, 31-60, 61+ days)
- Average collection period
- Problem accounts requiring follow-up
- Collection effectiveness

**4. Accounts Payable Analysis**
- Current payables due
- Upcoming payment obligations
- Payment terms analysis
- Vendor payment history
- Early payment opportunities (discounts)

**5. Inventory & Work in Progress (if applicable)**
- Unbilled work in progress
- Project completion status
- Billable milestones pending
- Capital tied up in ongoing work

**6. Cash Conversion Cycle**
- Days sales outstanding (DSO)
- Days payable outstanding (DPO)
- Cash conversion cycle length
- Trend vs previous periods
- Optimization opportunities

**7. Short-Term Liquidity**
- Quick ratio
- Current ratio
- Days cash on hand
- Ability to cover immediate obligations
- Liquidity stress indicators

**8. Weekly Cash Movements**
- Operating cash flow
- Investing cash flow
- Financing cash flow
- Net cash position change
- Comparison to budget/forecast

**9. Working Capital Trends**
- Week-over-week changes
- Month-to-date trends
- Year-over-year comparison (if available)
- Seasonal patterns
- Concerning trends

**10. Action Items & Recommendations**
- Receivables collection priorities
- Payment scheduling strategies
- Working capital optimization
- Early payment discount opportunities
- Cash preservation measures
- Growth investment opportunities

**Additional Notes:**
- Focus on operational efficiency insights
- Highlight opportunities to improve working capital
- Flag any liquidity stress or concerns
- Include vendor relationship considerations
- Support strategic decision-making

**Output Format:**
Comprehensive working capital report with clear metrics, trend analysis, and actionable recommendations for optimizing short-term financial position.

---
**Action Required:**
- Analyze current assets and liabilities
- Track receivables aging and collection patterns
- Review payable obligations and payment terms
- Calculate working capital ratios and metrics
- Assess cash conversion cycle efficiency
- Identify optimization opportunities
- Generate weekly working capital report
- Provide strategic recommendations

**Use:** Working capital optimization, liquidity planning, operational efficiency

**Priority:** High - critical for operational health and financial efficiency
