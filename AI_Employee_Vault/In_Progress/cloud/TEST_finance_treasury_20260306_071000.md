# Task: Weekly Treasury & Cash Position Report

**Task Type:** FINANCE - TREASURY REPORT

**Report Period:** Week of March 3-7, 2026

**Purpose:**
Generate a weekly treasury report to monitor cash positions, manage liquidity, optimize fund allocation, and ensure adequate resources for operations and growth.

**Report Sections Needed:**

**1. Treasury Overview**
- Total cash and cash equivalents
- Cash distribution across accounts
- Available credit facilities
- Total accessible liquidity
- Overall treasury health status

**2. Cash Position by Account**
- Operating account balance
- Reserve/savings account balance
- Business credit card available credit
- Lines of credit available
- PayPal/Stripe balances (if applicable)
- Total liquid assets

**3. Cash Flow Analysis (Week)**
- Opening balance (Monday)
- Total cash received
- Total cash deployed
- Net cash movement
- Closing balance (Friday)
- Variance from forecast

**4. Receivables Pipeline**
- Invoices issued this week
- Payments received
- Outstanding receivables total
- Expected collections next 7 days
- Expected collections next 30 days
- Collection probability assessment

**5. Payables Schedule**
- Payments due next 7 days
- Payments due next 30 days
- Critical payment dates
- Vendor payment priority matrix
- Early payment discount opportunities
- Cash outflow forecast

**6. Liquidity Assessment**
- Current ratio
- Quick ratio
- Days cash on hand
- Burn rate (if applicable)
- Runway months remaining
- Liquidity stress indicators

**7. Fund Allocation Strategy**
- Operating reserves target
- Growth/investment fund status
- Emergency fund adequacy
- Tax reserve status
- Optimized allocation recommendations

**8. Banking & Credit Facilities**
- Account utilization
- Credit line usage
- Available borrowing capacity
- Interest rates and fees
- Banking relationship health

**9. Risk Factors & Mitigation**
- Concentration risks (single client, single bank)
- Currency exposures (if applicable)
- Counterparty risks
- Market risks (interest rates)
- Mitigation strategies

**10. Strategic Recommendations**
- Cash deployment opportunities
- Reserve optimization
- Credit facility utilization
- Banking relationship improvements
- Treasury efficiency enhancements
- Risk mitigation actions

**Additional Notes:**
- Provide executive-level treasury insights
- Highlight opportunities for yield/return on cash
- Flag any liquidity concerns early
- Include regulatory compliance notes (if applicable)
- Support strategic financial planning

**Output Format:**
Professional treasury report with clear account breakdowns, risk assessments, and strategic recommendations for optimal cash management.

---
**Action Required:**
- Aggregate cash positions across all accounts
- Track receivables pipeline and payment probabilities
- Analyze payables schedule and prioritize payments
- Calculate liquidity ratios and metrics
- Assess fund allocation and optimization opportunities
- Review banking and credit facility utilization
- Generate comprehensive treasury report
- Provide strategic recommendations

**Use:** Treasury management, liquidity optimization, strategic cash allocation

**Priority:** High - essential for financial stability and growth planning
