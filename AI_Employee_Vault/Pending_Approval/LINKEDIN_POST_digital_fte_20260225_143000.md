# Your Best Employee Just Asked to Work Nights, Weekends, and Holidays...

And they said: "No salary. No benefits. No complaints."

Sounds too good to be true, right?

Meet the Digital FTE — a Full-Time Equivalent built from code, not coffee.

Traditional employee: $40K-$80K/year, works 40 hours, needs breaks, gets sick, takes vacations.

Digital FTE: Works 24/7/365, handles repetitive tasks perfectly, never burns out.

**What can a Digital FTE do?**
- Monitor your inbox 24/7 and draft responses
- Qualify leads while you sleep
- Generate and post content automatically
- Handle data entry and CRM updates
- Process invoices and follow up on payments

The businesses scaling fastest right now aren't hiring more people. They're building digital workforces.

Here's the truth: You don't need more hours in the day. You need an employee who never stops working.

## Key Takeaways
- Identify your top 3 repetitive tasks
- Calculate the hours wasted weekly
- Build or buy an AI agent to handle them

What's ONE task you'd offload to a 24/7 employee?
