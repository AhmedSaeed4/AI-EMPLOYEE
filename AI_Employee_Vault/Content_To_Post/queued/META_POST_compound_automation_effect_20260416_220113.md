Here's what I've noticed working with businesses on AI automation:

It's never ONE big thing that transforms a business.

It's 10 small things that each save 30 minutes a day.

That's 1,825 hours a year. An entire full-time employee — without hiring anyone.

None of these are revolutionary alone. But stack them together?

You just bought back 5+ hours a day to focus on what actually grows your business.
