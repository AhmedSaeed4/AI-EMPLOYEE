# Task: Weekly Liquidity & Cash Management Report

**Task Type:** FINANCE - LIQUIDITY REPORT

**Report Period:** Week of March 3-7, 2026

**Purpose:**
Generate a weekly liquidity report to monitor cash availability, track payment flows, and ensure sufficient funds for ongoing operations and upcoming expenses.

**Report Sections Needed:**

**1. Liquidity Overview**
- Current cash on hand
- Available credit lines
- Total liquid assets
- Liquidity status assessment (strong / adequate / tight)

**2. Cash Inflows This Week**
- Client payments received
- Amounts collected per client
- Payment method breakdown (wire, ACH, check)
- Total inflow amount
- Payments received ahead of schedule
- Late payments received

**3. Cash Outflows This Week**
- Vendor payments made
- Software and subscription fees
- Infrastructure and hosting costs
- Contractor and freelancer payments
- Operational expenses paid
- Total outflow amount

**4. Net Cash Movement**
- Inflow minus outflow
- Cash position change
- Comparison to previous week
- Trend analysis (improving / stable / declining)

**5. Upcoming Payment Obligations (Next 7 Days)**
- Bills due and amounts
- Invoice payment deadlines
- Scheduled expenses
- Contractual commitments
- Total upcoming outflow

**6. Expected Incoming Payments (Next 7 Days)**
- Invoices coming due
- Promised client payments
- Milestone billings
- Estimated inflow amount

**7. Outstanding Receivables**
- Total unpaid invoices
- Aging breakdown (0-30, 31-60, 61+ days)
- Problem accounts needing attention
- Collection efforts required

**8. Liquidity Health Metrics**
- Days of cash remaining (burn rate)
- Quick ratio calculation
- Current ratio (if applicable)
- Cash coverage for immediate expenses
- Any red flags or concerns

**9. Cash Flow Forecast**
- Projected cash position next week
- Potential shortfalls identified
- Surplus opportunities
- Recommended actions

**10. Recommendations**
- Invoice collection priorities
- Payment scheduling suggestions
- Expense timing adjustments
- Cash preservation measures (if needed)
- Growth or investment opportunities (if surplus)

**Additional Notes:**
- Highlight any urgent liquidity concerns
- Note significant variances from expectations
- Flag clients with payment issues
- Include actionable recommendations
- Support decision-making for cash management

**Output Format:**
Executive-friendly liquidity report with clear status indicators, key metrics highlighted, and prioritized action items.

---
**Action Required:**
- Retrieve current cash position and balances
- Track all cash inflows and outflows for the week
- Identify upcoming payment obligations and expected receipts
- Calculate liquidity health metrics
- Assess cash flow status and identify risks
- Generate weekly liquidity management report
- Provide actionable recommendations

**Use:** Cash flow monitoring, liquidity management, financial planning

**Priority:** High - essential for operational continuity and financial health
