# 3 Mistakes Killing Your AI Automation Before It Starts

You bought the AI tools. You watched the tutorials. You even built a few workflows.

So why does it feel like nothing changed? 😤

After working with businesses on AI implementation, I see the same 3 mistakes on repeat:

**Mistake #1: Automating broken processes**

If your lead follow-up takes 3 days manually, automating it won't fix the root problem. Fix the process FIRST, then let AI run it. AI amplifies whatever you give it — including inefficiency.

**Mistake #2: Going too broad, too fast**

Trying to automate 15 things at once means nothing works well. The businesses seeing real results? They picked ONE painful task, automated it completely, then moved to the next.

**Mistake #3: No human in the loop**

AI is powerful. But for financial decisions, client communications, and anything reputation-critical — you need a human checkpoint. The best automations run fast AND stay safe.

The fix is simpler than you think:
1. Pick your most expensive repetitive task
2. Map the process end to end
3. Automate it with a human approval step
4. Measure the time saved
5. Repeat

One task at a time. That's how you build an AI-powered business.

## Key Takeaways
- Don't automate broken processes — fix them first
- Start with ONE task, not fifteen
- Keep humans in the loop for sensitive decisions
