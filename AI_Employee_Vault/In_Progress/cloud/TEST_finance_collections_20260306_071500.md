# Task: Weekly Revenue & Collection Report

**Task Type:** FINANCE - REVENUE TRACKING

**Report Period:** Week of March 3-7, 2026

**Purpose:**
Generate a weekly revenue and collections report to track income, monitor billing activities, and optimize accounts receivable management.

**Report Sections Needed:**

**1. Revenue Summary**
- Total revenue recognized this week
- Revenue by project/client
- Revenue by service type (development, maintenance, consulting)
- Week-over-week comparison
- Year-to-date revenue total

**2. Invoicing Activity**
- New invoices created this week
- Total invoiced amount
- Invoices by client
- Invoice aging (created vs due dates)
- Upcoming invoice schedule

**3. Collections Activity**
- Payments received this week
- Total amount collected
- Payment breakdown by client
- Payment methods (wire, ACH, check, card)
- Collections vs forecast

**4. Accounts Receivable Status**
- Total outstanding receivables
- Aging breakdown:
  - Current (0-30 days)
  - Overdue (31-60 days)
  - Delinquent (61-90 days)
  - Critical (90+ days)
- Top overdue accounts
- Collection success rate

**5. Project Revenue Recognition**
- Projects completed this week
- Milestones achieved
- Billable hours vs target
- Revenue recognized per project
- Work in progress billing

**6. Client Revenue Analysis**
- Top revenue clients this week
- Client concentration (% of total revenue)
- New client revenue vs existing clients
- Client retention indicators

**7. Billing Efficiency Metrics**
- Average invoice amount
- Days to invoice (from work completion)
- Payment turnaround time
- Collection rate (% billed vs collected)
- Billing cycle efficiency

**8. Forecast vs Actual**
- Revenue forecast for the week
- Actual revenue vs forecast variance
- Collection forecast vs actual
- Explanation of significant variances

**9. Revenue Pipeline**
- Proposals/pending deals likely to close
- Expected revenue next 30 days
- Projects starting soon
- Upsell opportunities identified

**10. Action Items**
- Overdue invoice follow-ups
- Upcoming billing reminders
- Client payment terms to review
- Collections outreach needed
- Process improvements identified

**Additional Notes:**
- Highlight positive trends (strong collections, new client wins)
- Flag concerns (declining revenue, payment delays)
- Note any seasonal patterns
- Include recommendations for revenue optimization

**Output Format:**
Executive-friendly revenue report with clear metrics, trend analysis, and actionable collection strategies.

---
**Action Required:**
- Query Odoo for revenue and invoicing data
- Track collections and payment activities
- Analyze accounts receivable aging
- Calculate revenue metrics and trends
- Assess billing efficiency
- Generate weekly revenue & collections report
- Provide actionable recommendations

**Use:** Revenue tracking, collections management, financial forecasting

**Priority:** High - critical for cash flow and business growth
