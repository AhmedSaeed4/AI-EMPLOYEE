# An AI demo wins applause. AI running your business at 2am wins customers. 🎤➡️💰

Most businesses we talk to have "done AI."

They've got a slick demo. A chatbot that answers a few questions. A cool proof-of-concept that impressed the whole team in a meeting.

Then the meeting ends… and nothing actually runs. 🙃

Here's the gap nobody talks about: an AI that *can* do a task vs. an AI that *does* the task — reliably, every day, connected to your real tools, watched for errors, and approved by a human before anything risky goes out.

A demo is a magic trick. A Digital FTE is infrastructure. One gets applause. The other brings in leads while you sleep. 🌙

## Key Takeaways
- 🎬 A demo proves possibility — a deployed AI creates value
- ⚙️ "Connected to your real tools + human-in-the-loop" is where ROI actually lives
- 🏆 The best AI employee isn't the smartest one — it's the one working at 2am

If your AI lives in a slide deck instead of your workflow, you've got a demo, not a teammate.
