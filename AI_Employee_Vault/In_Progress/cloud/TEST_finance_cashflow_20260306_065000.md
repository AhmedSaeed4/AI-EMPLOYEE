# Task: Weekly Cash Flow Analysis

**Task Type:** FINANCE - CASH FLOW REPORT

**Report Period:** Week of March 3-7, 2026

**Purpose:**
Generate a focused weekly cash flow report to monitor liquidity, upcoming expenses, and ensure healthy cash position for business operations.

**Report Sections Needed:**

**1. Cash Position Summary**
- Starting cash balance (Monday morning)
- Ending cash balance (Friday afternoon)
- Net change for the week
- Cash reserve status (healthy / adequate / concerning)

**2. Cash In (Inflows)**
- Payments received from clients
- New invoice payments
- Payment breakdown by client
- Total cash collected this week
- Payments received vs expected (any delays?)

**3. Cash Out (Outflows)**
- Expenses paid this week
- Contractor payments
- Software subscriptions (GLM, hosting, tools)
- Infrastructure costs (AWS, servers)
- Marketing expenses
- Other operational expenses
- Total cash outflow

**4. Upcoming Cash Commitments (Next 7 Days)**
- Invoices due for payment
- Scheduled expenses
- Contractual obligations
- Estimated cash outflow next week

**5. Expected Cash In (Next 7 Days)**
- Overdue invoices (clients who promised payment)
- Pending payments in process
- Projects reaching billing milestones
- Estimated cash inflow next week

**6. Accounts Receivable Status**
- Total outstanding receivables
- Invoices overdue (with amounts and days)
- Clients to follow up with
- Expected payment dates

**7. Cash Flow Health Indicators**
- Quick ratio (cash / immediate expenses)
- Burn rate (if applicable)
- Runway (months of cash remaining)
- Any cash flow gaps or concerns

**8. Action Items**
- Urgent: Follow up on overdue invoices
- Payments to schedule or prioritize
- Cash flow concerns to address
- Clients to contact about payment terms
- Expenses to review or delay if needed

**9. Week-Over-Week Comparison**
- Cash collected vs previous week
- Expenses vs previous week
- Notable changes or trends
- Seasonal patterns (if any)

**Additional Notes:**
- Focus on actionable cash flow insights
- Flag any potential cash crunch scenarios
- Highlight positive developments (large payments received)
- Note any clients with payment issues
- Include recommendations for cash flow optimization

**Output Format:**
Concise cash flow report with clear status indicators,重点关注流动性风险 and actionable items.

---
**Action Required:**
- Query Odoo for cash flow data (inflows, outflows, balances)
- Compile AR/AP status and upcoming commitments
- Calculate cash flow health metrics
- Identify concerns, overdue items, action items
- Generate focused weekly cash flow analysis
- Provide recommendations for cash flow optimization

**Use:** Cash flow monitoring, liquidity management, financial planning

**Priority:** High - cash flow is critical for business operations
