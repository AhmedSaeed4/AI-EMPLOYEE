# 3 Automation Mistakes That Are Costing You Sales 🛑

You invested in automation to save time and boost revenue. But if you're making these 3 mistakes, you might be losing customers instead.

**Mistake #1: Automating the wrong things first.**
Most businesses start with internal workflows. Smart businesses start with customer-facing touchpoints — lead response, follow-ups, and support. Every minute a lead waits is a minute they're shopping elsewhere.

**Mistake #2: No human handoff.**
Bots handle the routine. But when a customer hits a real problem, they need a human. If your automation doesn't know when to escalate, you're creating frustration, not efficiency.

**Mistake #3: Set it and forget it.**
Automation isn't a crockpot. You can't just turn it on and walk away. Customer needs change. Your automation should too. Review monthly. Tweak quarterly. Iterate always.

## Key Takeaways
- Start with customer-facing automation, not internal tasks
- Always build in a human escalation path
- Review and refine your automation regularly

The difference between automation that scales your business and automation that hurts it? Strategy.
