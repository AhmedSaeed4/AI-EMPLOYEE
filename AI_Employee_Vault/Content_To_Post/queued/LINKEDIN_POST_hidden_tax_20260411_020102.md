# Your Business Has a Tax You've Never Heard Of

It's not on your P&L. Your accountant doesn't track it. But it's costing you more than any expense line item.

It's called the "Invisible Task Tax."

Every time you:
- Manually reply to a routine email
- Copy-paste data between tools that don't sync
- Follow up with a lead 2 days late because you forgot
- Spend 30 minutes writing a social post from scratch

You're paying a tax. In time. In momentum. In lost revenue.

Individually, these look like "just 10 minutes." But added up? Most small business owners I talk to are spending 3-4 hours EVERY DAY on work that doesn't require their expertise.

That's 1,000+ hours a year. Gone.

The businesses that are growing right now? They didn't find more hours in the day. They stopped paying the tax.

They automated the repetitive. Connected the disconnected. And freed themselves to do the work that actually moves revenue.

## Key Takeaways
- Track your tasks for one week — highlight everything that didn't need YOUR brain specifically
- The biggest cost isn't what you spend — it's the revenue you never earn because you're stuck in operations
- You don't need to automate everything. Start with the ONE thing you dread doing every morning
