# I Built an Employee That Works 24/7 (And It's Not What You Think)

Last month, I started experimenting with something that changed how I think about work.

I call them Digital FTEs - AI employees that never sleep, never take breaks, and cost a fraction of a traditional hire.

Here's what surprised me:

It wasn't the technology that was hard. It was figuring out what tasks to delegate.

The businesses getting the most value from AI aren't the ones with the fanciest tools. They're the ones who:

1. **Map their workflows first** - Document what actually happens step-by-step
2. **Start with repetitive tasks** - Email sorting, data entry, follow-ups
3. **Build trust incrementally** - Let the AI handle small tasks, then expand

I've been building these systems for myself and clients. The pattern is always the same:

First week: Skepticism
Second week: Curiosity
Third week: "How did I ever live without this?"

The future isn't AI replacing humans. It's AI handling the work that drains your energy, so you can focus on what actually moves the needle.

## Key Takeaways
- Start by mapping your current workflows
- Delegate one repetitive task to AI this week
- Think of AI as a team member, not a tool
