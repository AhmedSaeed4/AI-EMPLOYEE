# The #1 Mistake Businesses Make with AI

Stop thinking of AI as a tool.

Start thinking of it as an employee.

Most businesses treat AI like a fancy calculator — they ask it questions, get answers, and move on. But the real competitive advantage? Building AI that works like a full-time team member.

Here's what a Digital FTE actually does:
- Monitors your inbox and drafts responses
- Tracks leads and follows up automatically
- Generates social media content on schedule
- Logs every action so nothing falls through the cracks

It doesn't take sick days. It doesn't forget follow-ups. It doesn't need micromanagement.

The businesses that figure this out first won't just save time — they'll scale operations that used to require an entire department.

## Key Takeaways
- AI as a "tool" = limited ROI. AI as an "employee" = exponential ROI
- The shift is mental, not technical — start by defining the "job description"
- 24/7 coverage means leads never go cold and tasks never pile up
