# Ask Yourself This One Question at 5 PM Today ⏰

At the end of your workday, look at everything you did and ask:

**"If I had to do today over again, how much of it could a machine handle?"**

Most business owners we talk to are shocked by the answer.

They realize 40-60% of their day was spent on things that don't require human judgment — replying to routine emails, updating spreadsheets, scheduling posts, organizing files, chasing follow-ups.

Not because these tasks aren't important. They are.

But because they don't need YOU to do them.

## The 3-Step Test

1. **Track tomorrow** — Write down every task you do and how long it takes
2. **Tag each one** — "Needs my brain" or "Could be automated"
3. **Count the hours** — That number is your automation opportunity

The businesses that scale aren't the ones working harder. They're the ones who figured out what to stop doing themselves.

## Key Takeaways
- 40-60% of most workdays are automatable tasks
- Awareness is the first step — track before you change anything
- The goal isn't to work less, it's to work on what actually matters

What would your number be? Be honest in the comments — no judgment here.
