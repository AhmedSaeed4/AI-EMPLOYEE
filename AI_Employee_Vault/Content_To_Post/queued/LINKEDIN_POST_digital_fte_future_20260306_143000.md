# Your Best Employee Never Sleeps

Here's the uncomfortable truth about scaling a business today:

Hiring more people doesn't always mean getting more done.

More employees = more management overhead, more training, more coordination, more headaches.

But what if you could hire someone who:
✅ Works 24/7/365
✅ Never calls in sick
✅ Doesn't need benefits
✅ Scales infinitely
✅ Costs a fraction of a traditional hire

Meet the Digital FTE.

A Digital FTE (Full-Time Equivalent) is an AI employee that handles specific business functions autonomously:
- Customer support (at 2 AM when your human team is offline)
- Lead qualification and follow-up
- Content scheduling and posting
- Data processing and reporting

The businesses scaling fastest aren't hiring 50 people. They're hiring 10 humans + 10 Digital FTEs that multiply their output 10x.

**The future isn't AI replacing humans.**
It's humans + AI, working in parallel.

The question isn't "Will AI take my job?"
The question is "How will I use AI to do my job better?"

## Key Takeaways
- Identify repetitive tasks that drain your team's energy
- Start with ONE function to automate (support, sales, admin)
- Measure output per human, not total headcount
