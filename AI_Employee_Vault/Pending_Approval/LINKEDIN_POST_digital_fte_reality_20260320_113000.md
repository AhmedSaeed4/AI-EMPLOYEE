# I replaced a $60,000/year employee with a $500/month AI agent. Here's what happened:

Spoiler: The business didn't collapse. It grew 3x.

Here's what most people get wrong about AI employees:

They're not here to replace your best people. They're here to handle the work that's burning them out.

The repetitive tasks. The data entry. The follow-ups that fall through the cracks. The 11 PM "urgent" requests.

A Digital FTE doesn't:
- Call in sick
- Need benefits
- Ask for raises
- Get tired at 3 PM

It does:
- Work 24/7
- Follow processes perfectly
- Scale instantly
- Cost 90% less

The real question isn't "Can AI replace my team?"

It's "What could my team achieve if they weren't drowning in busywork?"

One of our clients freed up 20 hours/week of their sales team's time. Those hours went to closing deals instead of chasing data.

Revenue up. Team morale up. Burnout down.

That's the AI employee advantage.

## Key Takeaways
- AI employees handle repetitive work so your team can focus on high-value tasks
- The cost savings are real, but the productivity gains are bigger
- Your best people become even more valuable when they're not drowning in admin
