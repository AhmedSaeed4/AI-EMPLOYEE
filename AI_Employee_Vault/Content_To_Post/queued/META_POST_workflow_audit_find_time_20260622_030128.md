# Most businesses aren't drowning in work — they're drowning in copy-paste 📋

You don't need more hours. You need to find the hours quietly leaking out the back door.

Here's the 5-minute audit we run before we build anything for a client 👇

## The 3 questions that find your biggest time leak

1. **"What do I copy from one place and paste into another?"**
   Every copy-paste is a handoff a machine should be making.

2. **"What do I do the exact same way every Monday, Friday, or month-end?"**
   Repetition is the loudest signal that a task *wants* to be automated.

3. **"What breaks the moment someone is sick or on vacation?"**
   If it lives in one person's head, it's a risk — not a process.

Pick the ONE answer that showed up in all three. That's your first task to hand to an AI employee. Not the flashiest one. The boring, repeatable, everywhere-one. That single handoff usually pays for the rest.

## Why this works

You're not automating for fun. You're buying back a full workday every week — without hiring, without onboarding, and without it ever calling in sick. 🤖

## Key Takeaways
- Copy-paste = a handoff a machine should be making
- Repetition on a schedule = your best automation candidate
- Automate the boring-everywhere task first, not the impressive one
- One good handoff can fund your entire AI setup
