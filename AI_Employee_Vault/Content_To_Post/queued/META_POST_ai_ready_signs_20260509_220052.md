# 3 Signs Your Business Is Ready for AI (And 1 That Means Wait) 🤖

Not every business needs AI right now. Here's how to know if you actually do:

**1. You're repeating the same task 5+ times a week**

If you're writing the same type of email, pulling the same report, or answering the same questions daily — you're a prime candidate. AI thrives on patterns. Repetition is its superpower.

**2. Growth is bottlenecked by bandwidth, not demand**

You're getting leads. You're getting requests. But you simply can't keep up without burning out. That's not a "hustle harder" problem — that's an automation opportunity.

**3. Your best people are doing their worst work**

When your creative team is formatting spreadsheets or your strategist is scheduling posts, something's broken. AI handles the grind so humans handle the genius.

**⚠️ The "Wait" Sign:**

You don't have a clear process yet. If the task is different every time and lives entirely in someone's head, automate the chaos = chaos at scale. Document first. Automate second.

## Key Takeaways
- Repetitive tasks = AI's sweet spot
- Bandwidth bottlenecks are automation opportunities
- Free your team from low-value work
- But document your process BEFORE you automate
