# Why Your Reels Get 50 Views (And How to Fix It)

You spent hours editing that reel. You picked the perfect song. You even added captions. And yet... 50 views. Maybe 60 if your mom shared it. 😅

Here's the truth: most reels fail before the first second even plays. The algorithm doesn't hate you — your hook just isn't doing its job.

**The 3-Second Rule:**
Viewers decide in 3 seconds whether to keep watching or scroll. That means your opening frame needs to do ONE of these things:

1️⃣ **Show, don't tell** — Open with a striking visual, not a talking head. A 3D animation, a bold text overlay, or a before/after transformation grabs attention instantly.

2️⃣ **Start with the result** — Show the finished product FIRST, then reveal how you got there. People stay to learn the "how."

3️⃣ **Pattern interrupt** — Break expectations. If everyone in your niche does talking head videos, do something visually different. Motion graphics, 2D effects, kinetic text — anything that disrupts the scroll.

**The format hack nobody talks about:**
Your best-performing content won't be what you think. Test 3 formats consistently:
- Educational (tips, tutorials)
- Entertainment (behind-the-scenes, fails)
- Visual showcase (your best work, no talking)

Post 1 of each every week. After a month, double down on whichever format wins. Simple, but almost nobody does this because it requires consistency.

## Key Takeaways
- Your first 3 seconds decide everything — invest your energy there
- Visual hooks outperform verbal hooks every time
- Consistency beats perfection — test 3 formats and double down on the winner
