# The bottleneck in your business isn't your team. It's you.

Most owners I talk to want to scale. But they've quietly become the single point of failure — every decision routes through them, every quote waits on their approval, every new lead sits untouched until they have a "free minute."

The business can only move as fast as you personally clear your inbox.

That's not a growth strategy. That's a speed limit with your name on it.

The fix isn't working harder, or hiring someone you'll spend three months training. It's pulling yourself out of the loop on the work that doesn't actually need your judgment:

→ Lead replies that should be instant, not "when I get back to my desk"
→ Follow-ups that slip through because *you* forgot, not the customer
→ Quotes and invoices that pile up at 5pm and steal tomorrow's morning

An AI employee doesn't replace your judgment. It handles everything *around* your judgment — so your judgment goes further, and your business stops waiting on you to find a free minute.

What's the one task you keep saying "I'll just do it myself, it's faster"?
