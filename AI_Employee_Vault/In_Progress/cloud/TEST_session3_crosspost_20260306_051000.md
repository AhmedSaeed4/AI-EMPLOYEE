# Task: Social Media Content Package - Multi-Platform Campaign

**Task Type:** SOCIAL MEDIA - CONTENT PACKAGE

**Campaign:** "5 AI Tools That Saved Our Client 15 Hours/Week"

**Campaign Overview:**
Create a coordinated content package sharing a client success story across multiple platforms. Each platform gets customized content optimized for its format and audience.

**Core Story:**
Client: GreenLeaf Landscaping (can use real name)
Problem: Drowning in admin work (scheduling, invoicing, follow-ups)
Solution: 3 simple automations (Zapier workflows + email sequences)
Result: Saved 15 hours/week, scaled to handle 5 more clients
Investment: $2,500 one-time setup

**Platform Requirements:**

### 1. LinkedIn (Long-form)
- **Format:** Article-style post (1,500-2,000 characters)
- **Focus:** Business impact, ROI, professional insights
- **Audience:** Business owners, decision makers
- **Tone:** Expert, data-driven, "here's what works"
- **Key stats to include:** 15 hours saved, 5 new clients, ROI timeline
- **CTA:** "What's ONE repetitive task you'd automate?"

### 2. Twitter/X (Thread)
- **Format:** 6-8 tweet thread
- **Focus:** Quick actionable insights, hook-driven
- **Audience:** Entrepreneurs, tech-savvy business owners
- **Tone:** Casual, punchy, thread-connector style
- **Structure:** Hook → Problem → Solution → Results → CTA
- **Use:** 🧵 emoji for thread indicator

### 3. Facebook (Story-based)
- **Format:** 200-400 characters
- **Focus:** Relatable storytelling, community engagement
- **Audience:** Local business owners, general small business community
- **Tone:** Warm, celebratory, "we're in this together"
- **Hook:** "They were working IN their business, not ON their business"
- **CTA:** Question about their biggest time-waster

### 4. Instagram (Visual-first)
- **Format:** Caption (150-250 characters)
- **Focus:** Aesthetic, behind-the-scenes, visual storytelling
- **Visual Suggestion:** Before/after graphic or "15 hours saved = what you can do" visual
- **Audience:** Young entrepreneurs, visual learners
- **Tone:** Authentic, aspirational, "this could be you"
- **Emojis:** Use sparingly but effectively (⏰✅📈)

**Campaign Notes:**
- All posts should go live within same 24-hour period for maximum impact
- Use consistent hashtags across platforms: #Automation #SmallBusiness #ProductivityHacks
- Cross-reference: "Follow for more business automation tips"
- Reply to all comments within 24 hours to boost algorithm
- Consider boosting/ promoting best-performing post

**Campaign Goals:**
- Generate 20+ qualified leads for automation services
- Position as automation expert for small businesses
- Build social proof with real client story
- Test which platform drives best engagement

**Success Metrics:**
- LinkedIn: 500+ impressions, 30+ reactions
- Twitter: 1,000+ impressions, 50+ likes
- Facebook: 200+ reach, 15+ reactions
- Instagram: 300+ reach, 25+ likes

**Hashtag Strategy:**
**Mix of:**
- Broad: #SmallBusiness #Automation #Productivity
- Niche: #SMBTech #BusinessAutomation #WorkSmarter
- Branded: Create unique hashtag if you have one

**Action Required:**
- Draft complete content package for all 4 platforms
- Each platform optimized for its format and audience
- Include relevant hashtags for each platform
- Suggest visual/creative for Instagram
- Provide posting schedule recommendation (which platform first?)
- Ensure consistent messaging but platform-specific execution

**Brand Voice:** Expert but accessible, results-focused, "here's what actually works" not theoretical advice
