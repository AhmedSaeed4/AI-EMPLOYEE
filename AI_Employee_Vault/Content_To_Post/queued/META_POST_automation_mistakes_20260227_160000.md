# 3 AI Automation Mistakes That Are Costing You Money 💸

Everyone's talking about AI automation. But here's what nobody tells you:

**Most businesses are doing it WRONG.**

And it's literally costing them money, time, and opportunities.

Let me break down the 3 biggest mistakes I see:

## Mistake #1: Automating BAD Processes 🚫

Before you automate anything, ask yourself: "Is this process actually WORTH automating?"

A broken process automated is still a broken process — it just breaks FASTER.

Fix the process first. THEN bring in AI.

## Mistake #2: The "Set It and Forget It" Trap 🤖

AI isn't magic. It needs monitoring, tweaking, and optimization.

The businesses winning with AI? They're constantly refining their automation.

They're not passive. They're ACTIVE.

## Mistake #3: One-Size-Fits-All Tools 📦

Generic AI tools give generic results.

Your business is unique. Your automation should be too.

Custom AI agents built for YOUR specific workflows = WAY better results.

## Key Takeaways
- Fix broken processes BEFORE automating them
- Monitor and optimize your AI systems constantly
- Custom solutions beat generic tools every time
