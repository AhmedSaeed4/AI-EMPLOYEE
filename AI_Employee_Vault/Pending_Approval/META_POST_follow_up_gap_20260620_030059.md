# Your leads aren't cold. Your follow-up is. 🥶

Here's an uncomfortable truth most businesses never admit:

Your prospects were interested. They filled out the form. They replied to your message. Then… life happened. A meeting ran long. A reminder got buried. A "let me think about it" turned into silence.

And just like that, a warm lead quietly froze.

The problem was never the product. It was never the price. It was the **gap** between their first yes and your next touch.

Most sales don't happen on contact #1. They happen somewhere around touch #5 to #8. But the average business gives up after #1 or #2. That gap is where the majority of your revenue is leaking right now — not from lost deals, but from **forgotten ones**.

Manual follow-up fails for three predictable reasons:
1. It's boring, so it gets deprioritized
2. It relies on memory, and memory has holes
3. It feels pushy, so people procrastinate it

That's exactly the kind of work a Digital FTE was built for. It follows up relentlessly, never forgets a name, never feels awkward, and never lets a warm lead cool off while you're "busy."

## Key Takeaways
- Most revenue leaks from dropped follow-up, not bad products
- Deals usually close at touch #5–8 — most quit by #2
- The fix isn't more hustle, it's a system that never forgets
