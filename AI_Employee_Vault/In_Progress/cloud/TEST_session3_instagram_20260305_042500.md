**Content Request:** Create an Instagram behind-the-scenes post showing the development workspace

Share a glimpse into where the magic happens. Make it relatable to other developers and entrepreneurs who understand the hustle.

**Goals:**

- Build connection with audience
- Show authenticity

- Not too polished or corporate
- Show personality
- Acknowledge the grind in a positive way

**Visual Details:**

**Caption Length:** Aim for 150-250 characters (Instagram sweet spot)
