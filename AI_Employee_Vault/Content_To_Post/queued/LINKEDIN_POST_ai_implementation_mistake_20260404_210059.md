# The #1 Mistake Businesses Make When Adopting AI

They try to automate everything at once.

I see it all the time. A business owner discovers AI, gets excited, and wants to build 15 automations by Friday.

Result? Nothing works. They get frustrated. They write off AI as "not ready yet."

Here's the approach that actually works:

**Pick ONE painful, repetitive workflow. Automate just that.**

Not your entire operations. Not your whole sales pipeline. Just one thing that eats 2-3 hours of your day.

Example: A client came to us drowning in lead follow-ups. We didn't rebuild their entire system. We built ONE AI agent that handles initial outreach and qualification.

That single agent saved them 12 hours a week and helped them respond to leads 10x faster.

**When one automation proves its value, you layer on the next one. And the next.**

Before you know it, you've got a digital workforce running in the background — built one brick at a time.

## Key Takeaways
- Start with one workflow, not your entire business
- Prove ROI on one automation before building the next
- Small wins build momentum — big bangs build nothing
