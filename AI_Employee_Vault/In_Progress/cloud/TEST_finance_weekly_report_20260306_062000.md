# Task: Generate Weekly Financial Report

**Task Type:** FINANCE - WEEKLY REPORT

**Report Period:** Week of February 24 - March 2, 2026

**Purpose:**
Generate a comprehensive weekly financial report to review business performance, track cash flow, and identify any issues requiring attention.

**Report Sections Needed:**

**1. Revenue Summary**
- Total revenue for the week
- Revenue by client/project
- Comparison to previous week
- Revenue by service type (web development, maintenance, consulting)
- Year-to-date revenue total

**2. Expense Summary**
- Total expenses for the week
- Major expense categories (software, infrastructure, contractor payments)
- Any unusual or one-time expenses
- Comparison to budget (if available)

**3. Accounts Receivable**
- Outstanding invoices and aging
- Total amount pending
- Invoices past due (categorized by days overdue)
- Clients who haven't paid according to terms

**4. Cash Flow**
- Cash in (payments received)
- Cash out (expenses paid)
- Net cash position for the week
- Any cash flow concerns or upcoming large expenses

**5. Invoice Activity**
- New invoices created this week
- Invoices paid this week
- Total invoiced vs collected
- Payment status summary

**6. Key Metrics**
- Average invoice amount
- Average payment time (days)
- Revenue per active client
- Any trends or notable changes

**7. Action Items**
- Follow-up on overdue invoices
- Clients to contact about upcoming renewals
- Expenses requiring approval or review
- Opportunities or concerns to address

**Additional Notes:**
- This report should be formatted for easy reading by business leadership
- Include both summary highlights and detailed breakdowns
- Highlight any items requiring immediate attention
- Note any unusual patterns or significant changes from previous weeks
- Suggest any financial decisions or actions based on the data

**Output Format:**
Professional report format with clear sections, bullet points for key items, and summary at the top.

---
**Action Required:**
- Query Odoo accounting system for weekly financial data
- Compile revenue, expense, accounts receivable, and cash flow information
- Generate formatted weekly financial report
- Highlight any concerns, overdue items, or action items
- Calculate key metrics and trends
- Present in clear, executive-friendly format

**Use:** Review with leadership team for business insights and financial health monitoring

**Priority:** Medium - routine weekly report, but timely information is valuable
