# Task: Facebook Post - Client Success Story

**Task Type:** SOCIAL MEDIA POST

**Platform:** Facebook

**Content Request:** Create a Facebook post sharing a client success story

**Client Story:**
**Client:** GreenLeaf Landscaping (local small business)
**Challenge:** Spending 15+ hours/week on manual scheduling and invoicing
**Solution:** Automated scheduling system + automated invoice reminders
**Result:** Saved 12 hours/week, got 5 more clients with freed-up time

**Post Structure:**
- Hook: Relatable small business struggle
- Story: GreenLeaf's before/after
- Proof: Concrete numbers (12 hours saved)
- Lesson: Automation isn't just for big companies
- CTA: Question to engage comments

**Key Message:**
"Small businesses deserve the same automation advantages as big companies. GreenLeaf went from drowning in admin work to growing their business - all because they automated the repetitive stuff."

**Target Audience:**
- Small business owners
- Local business owners
- People feeling overwhelmed by admin work

**Goals:**
- Build credibility with social proof
- Attract similar small business clients
- Generate comments and engagement
- Position as automation expert for SMBs

**Tone:** Warm, storytelling, celebratory, not salesy
- Focus on the client's success
- Show you understand small business pain
- Be authentic and relatable

**Visual Suggestion:**
"Photo of GreenLeaf team or before/after graphic showing 'hours spent on admin'"

**Hashtags:** #SmallBusinessSuccess #Automation #SmallBiz #BusinessTips #LocalBusiness

**Character Guidelines:**
- Facebook allows longer posts (up to 63,206 characters)
- Aim for 200-400 characters for optimal engagement
- Use emoji naturally (not overdone)
- Break into short paragraphs for readability

**Action Required:**
- Draft engaging Facebook post
- Tell the story clearly and concisely
- Include specific results (numbers work well)
- End with question to drive comments
- Add relevant hashtags (3-5 max)
- Suggest a visual to accompany

**Brand Voice:** Celebrate clients, authentic, "we're in this together", expertise without arrogance
