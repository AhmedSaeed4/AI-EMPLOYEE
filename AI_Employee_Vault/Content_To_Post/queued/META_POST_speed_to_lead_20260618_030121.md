# The fastest reply wins. The second-fastest loses the lead. ⏱️

Here's a stat that should keep every business owner up at night: reply to a new lead within the first 5 minutes and you're up to 21x more likely to qualify them.

Wait longer? You're not "busy." You're handing that customer to whoever answered first. 📉

Most solos and small teams can't live in their inbox 24/7. Leads come in at dinner, mid-shoot, at 2am. And every missed minute is money walking out the door.

This is exactly where a Digital FTE changes the game. It answers instantly, qualifies the lead, and books the next step — while you're editing, sleeping, or building something new. No human has to babysit it. 🤖

But answering fast only matters if people reach out in the first place. That's the other half: visuals that make someone STOP scrolling and send that message — custom 3D, motion, a look that's unmistakably yours. 🎬

Fast replies bring the lead in. Scroll-stopping visuals get them to knock. Put both on autopilot and you don't just compete — you vanish from the competition's rearview mirror. 🚀

## Key Takeaways
- Replying within 5 minutes makes you up to 21x more likely to convert a lead
- A Digital FTE answers and qualifies instantly — no inbox babysitting required
- Speed closes the leads; custom visuals create them. You need both to win
