# I Replaced 3 Software Subscriptions with ONE AI Employee

Most businesses are paying for:
- An email automation tool ($50/mo)
- A social media scheduler ($30/mo)
- A CRM with follow-up sequences ($70/mo)

That's $1,800/year for tools that each do ONE thing.

And they still can't talk to each other.

Here's what changed when I built a Digital FTE instead:

**1. Email triage — handled**
My AI reads incoming emails, flags urgent ones, and drafts responses. No more $50/mo tool that just sends auto-replies.

**2. Social media — handled**
Content creation, scheduling, posting. Across platforms. One system. No more logging into 3 different dashboards.

**3. Lead follow-up — handled**
New lead comes in? Response within 60 seconds. Personalized. Logged in the CRM. No lead goes cold.

The real win isn't the money saved. It's that everything is connected now.

One system. One workflow. Zero gaps where leads fall through.

## Key Takeaways
- Audit your SaaS stack — if tools don't integrate, you're paying for silos
- A Digital FTE doesn't replace your tools — it replaces the disconnect between them
- The ROI isn't just cost savings — it's the revenue from leads that never slip through again
