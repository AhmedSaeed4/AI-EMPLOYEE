# The $50K Employee That Never Sleeps, Never Takes PTO, and Works 24/7 🚀

What if I told you that for a fraction of the cost of one full-time hire, you could have an entire team that works around the clock?

I'm talking about Digital FTEs — AI employees that handle the repetitive, time-consuming tasks that drain your team's energy and your budget.

Here's what a Digital FTE can do for your business:

✅ Answer customer inquiries instantly (no more "we'll get back to you in 24 hours")
✅ Process data, generate reports, and manage workflows while you sleep
✅ Handle lead qualification so your sales team only talks to hot prospects
✅ Scale up or down instantly based on demand — no hiring cycles, no layoffs

One of our clients replaced 15 hours of manual data entry per week with a single AI agent. That's nearly HALF an employee's worth of time... freed up to focus on growth.

The businesses winning in 2026 aren't hiring more people. They're augmenting their people with AI that handles the busy work.

## Key Takeaways
- Digital FTEs work 24/7 at a fraction of human employee costs
- AI agents excel at repetitive tasks like data entry, lead qualification, and customer support
- Scaling becomes instant — no recruitment cycles or training periods
- Your human team can focus on strategy and creativity while AI handles execution
