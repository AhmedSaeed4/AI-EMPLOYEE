# 3 Myths About AI Employees That Are Costing You Growth

Everyone's talking about AI. But most businesses I talk to are held back by the same misconceptions.

**Myth 1: "AI will replace my entire team"**
Reality: The best AI employees handle the repetitive 60% so your human team can focus on the high-value 40%. It's augmentation, not replacement.

**Myth 2: "AI automation is only for big companies"**
Reality: Small businesses benefit the most. When you're a team of 5, getting an AI employee that handles email triage, lead follow-ups, and social posting 24/7 isn't a luxury — it's a lifeline.

**Myth 3: "Setting up AI is too complicated"**
Reality: A well-designed AI agent can be deployed in days, not months. The key is starting with one workflow that eats up your time and expanding from there.

The businesses winning right now aren't the ones waiting for AI to get "perfect." They're the ones starting small, learning fast, and scaling what works.
