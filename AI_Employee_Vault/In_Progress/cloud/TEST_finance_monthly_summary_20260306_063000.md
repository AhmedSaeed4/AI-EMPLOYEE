# Task: Generate Monthly Financial Summary

**Task Type:** FINANCE - MONTHLY SUMMARY

**Report Period:** February 2026

**Purpose:**
Generate a comprehensive monthly financial summary to review business performance, assess progress against goals, and plan for the month ahead.

**Report Sections Needed:**

**1. Executive Summary**
- Key highlights for the month
- Total revenue, expenses, profit
- Month-over-month growth
- Any significant achievements or concerns

**2. Revenue Analysis**
- Total revenue for February
- Revenue breakdown by client (top clients)
- Revenue by service type (web development, maintenance, consulting, retainers)
- Comparison to January (growth %)
- Year-to-date revenue (Jan + Feb)
- Revenue forecast for March based on pipeline

**3. Expense Analysis**
- Total expenses for February
- Major expense categories:
  - Software subscriptions (GLM, hosting, tools)
  - Infrastructure (AWS, server costs)
  - Contractor payments
  - Marketing and business development
  - Administrative expenses
- Any unusual or one-time expenses
- Month-over-month expense changes
- Budget variance (if applicable)

**4. Profitability**
- Gross profit for February
- Net profit margin (%)
- Profit per project/client
- Most and least profitable services

**5. Accounts Receivable & Payable**
- Outstanding invoices (total amount, count)
- Aging breakdown (30, 60, 90+ days)
- Clients with overdue invoices
- Upcoming bills to pay

**6. Cash Flow**
- Starting cash balance (Feb 1)
- Cash in (payments received)
- Cash out (expenses, contractor payments)
- Ending cash balance (Feb 28)
- Cash flow trend (positive/negative)

**7. Business Insights**
- Best-performing services or clients
- Areas for improvement
- Pricing observations
- Client retention status

**8. March Outlook**
- Confirmed projects starting in March
- Pipeline opportunities
- Anticipated expenses
- Revenue target for March

**9. Recommendations**
- Actions to improve profitability
- Clients to follow up with
- Expenses to optimize
- Growth opportunities to pursue

**Additional Notes:**
- Include visual comparisons (Feb vs Jan, YTD progress)
- Highlight any red flags or areas needing immediate attention
- Note any assumptions or estimates used
- Format for presentation to stakeholders

**Output Format:**
Professional monthly report with clear sections, key metrics highlighted, and actionable insights.

---
**Action Required:**
- Query Odoo for February financial data
- Compile revenue, expenses, profitability, AR/AP, cash flow
- Calculate month-over-month changes and YTD totals
- Identify trends, insights, and recommendations
- Generate executive-friendly monthly summary
- Include March forecast and action items

**Use:** Monthly business review, strategic planning, stakeholder updates

**Priority:** High - important for business planning and decision-making
